Hina Sasuga — Music Transcription

Agentic music transcription for a music teacher — turning audio performances into accurate sheet music with AI.

Client: Hina Sasuga
Music
Transcription Agent

Hina Sasuga is a music teacher who spends hours transcribing student performances by ear. She came to me with a simple question: can AI do this faster and more accurately?

We're building an agentic transcription system that listens to audio — whether it's a piano lesson recording, a vocal performance, or a full ensemble — and produces clean, editable sheet music. The agent handles pitch detection, rhythm analysis, and notation formatting.

For Hina, this means less time at a desk with headphones and more time actually teaching. The agent doesn't just transcribe — it learns the nuances of her students' playing styles over time.
