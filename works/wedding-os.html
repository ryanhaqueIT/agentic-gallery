Wedding OS (Emma)

AI studio manager built for Arzu Khanna & Parisha Rabadia — turning their wedding business idea into a fully agentic product.

Client: Arzu & Parisha
AI Product
Voice Agent
Architecture overview

Arzu Khanna and Parisha Rabadia came to me with a business idea: a smarter way for wedding vendors to manage leads without drowning in admin. Every inquiry is a thread — email, Instagram DM, phone call — and most go cold because the vendor is busy shooting.

Emma is the AI studio manager we built together. She handles the entire lead lifecycle: responding to new inquiries within minutes, qualifying them, answering FAQs, sending pricing, following up, and booking consultation calls — all in the vendor's own voice.

The system integrates email, voice (inbound and outbound), and a CRM into a single agentic workflow. Vendors don't learn a new tool. They just stop losing leads.
