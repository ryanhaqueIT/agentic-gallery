FinOps Remediation Orchestrator

Automated cloud cost optimization — turning FinOps insights into deployed infrastructure changes with AI-assisted remediation.

Enterprise
FinOps
Infrastructure Automation

This initiative is projecting to save $100K per month — that's $1.2 million per year in realized cloud cost savings, with the drive to scale to $800K per month across the bank. But the dollar savings are only half the story. The real unlock is time: what used to take an engineer 4–6 hours of manual remediation per recommendation now takes 15 minutes of review.

The gap between FinOps insight and engineering action was killing optimization adoption. Manual remediation workflows, slow implementation cycles, low adoption of recommendations, fragmented audit tracking, and operational risk during manual changes meant most savings opportunities expired before anyone touched them.

The Automated FinOps Remediation Orchestrator closes that gap. It ingests optimization recommendations from analytics platforms, enriches them with ownership and environment metadata, uses AI to generate the correct infrastructure-as-code changes, and submits pull requests with estimated savings, risk assessment, and rollback instructions — all automatically. Every change carries a full audit trail, and the engineering team reviews a 15-minute PR instead of spending half a day on manual rightsizing.
